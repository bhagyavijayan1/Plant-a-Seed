The Work in the
tree nursery

The seedlings grow strong in
our own tree nursery

We produce 20% more seedlings
than we need, so we can select
the best trees and keep our high
survival rate on the planting ground.
With 35 workers, we are growing
8 tree species.Each species sprouts
at a different time, so we schedule
when to seed each species.

Early Summer Until Winter

We take care of all the trees we have planted.

It is really important to clear the area of weeds on a regular basis so that the trees can grow freely. We need to clean the area every 3 months so the trees get enough sunlight and we keep our 94% survival rate.
